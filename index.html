A Quick Guide to Blitz

  • Draw 8 cards to start.
  • Make sets of 3 to match.
  • Eliminate the leader.

Role of die equals number of cards drawn and ma. number of actions (placing a card but also removing a card from the War Zone counts as an action)

Building: Return 3 cards from the discard pile to your hand OR return 6 cards from the top of the Arsenal.

Intel: Bounce 3 cards in teh War Zone or discard 3 cards from their hand or discard 6 from the top of the Arsenal (if Arsenal is depleted, Leader is compromised).

Force: Eliminate 1 territory (if all are eliminated, Leader is compromised). Only a Force match can eliminate a compromised Leader.

Morale: Opponent loses 6 morale or you gain 3 (if opponent's morale is 0, Leader is compromised).

Weather: Take 3 addititional actions or take an extra turn after this one.

WMD: Eliminate 2 territories (may only be played once, if all territores are eliminated, Leader is compromised - WMD can't eliminate a leader).
